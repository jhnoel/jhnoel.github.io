Eigenvalues and eigenvectors reveal fundamental properties of linear transformations.

## Definition

A non-zero vector $\mathbf{v}$ is an eigenvector of matrix $A$ with eigenvalue $\lambda$ if:

$$
A\mathbf{v} = \lambda\mathbf{v}
$$

## Applications

- Stability analysis of dynamical systems
- Principal component analysis (PCA)
- Spectral clustering
